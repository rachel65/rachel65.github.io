'Lace Before You Race' PSA

Created: January 28, 2015

This public service announcement was created in the course Web Design 2. The intended message is to always keep shoes tied, especially when participating in activities like racing, hence the saying "Lace before you race".

Flash techniques used include:

Multiple scenes
Used to enable use of multiple stage colors and backgrounds resulting in three total scenes
Bone tool
Animation involving multiple segments of a single stick figure were used, when the man fell in the race
Alpha properties
Text was faded in and out, and scenes were faded into, as a result of adjusting the alpha
Motion tweening
The racers, as well as the bow at the end, moved using this feature
