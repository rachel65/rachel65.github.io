Fossil Ridge High School Banner

Created: January 30, 2015

This banner was created in the course Web Design 2, following the guide pictured on the right. The intention was to create an animation that could be placed on a school's webpage as a an animated header.

Flash techniques used include:

Alpha properties
All images fade in and out of view as a result of adjusting the alpha
Motion tweening
Center images grow and move in from the left side
