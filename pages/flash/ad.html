Rocky Mountain Olive Oil Advertisement

Created: February 9, 2015

This advertisement banner was made in the course Web Design 2, following the guide pictured on the right. Rocky Mountain Olive Oil Company is a locally owned and operated business in Colorado and the animation is intended as something they could potentially place on third party sites to advertise their wide range of products.

Flash techniques used include:

Alpha properties
Text and images fade in and out of view as a result of adjusting the alpha
Motion tweening
Oil bottles slide into frame using this method of animation
