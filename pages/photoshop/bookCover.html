Fahrenheit 451 Book Cover

Created: March 11, 2015

This recreation of a book cover was created in the course Web Design 2. The novel is Fahrenheit 451 by author Ray Bradbury. The original image of the book cover can be found here.

Photoshop techniques used include:

Burn tool
Created a sort of shadow underneath the central image by darkening certain areas of the red background
Dodge tool
Lightly used around the dividing line and the 451 emblem to give some texture to the area
Pen tool
Used to outline sections of the original reference image
Selection tool (load selection)
The interior of certain letters and numbers had to be outlined and then selected by this tool to be erased
