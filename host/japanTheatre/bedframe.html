RECALL NOTICE
"The metal rod connecting the guard rail to the bed frame can break in use, posing a laceration hazard."

Date stamp: attached to either headboard or underside of bed
If you have an affected bed:
  • Stop use immediantly
  • Contact customer service at (888)-966-452 to aquire free repair kit
  • Replace metal rod and follow instructions in kit
The recall has affected more than 25,000 beds.
There have been only two reports of the metal rod breaking off in a home and no incidents involving the rod resulting in injury have been reported.

UPDATES
